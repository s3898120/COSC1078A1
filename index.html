Project Idea

Overview

For my project idea that i have developed, is an app that when other people text/call you, sends a replied message back letting them know that you're driving at the moment. For this project intend this proposed user type will be young people aged between 18-24 years old as they will typically living a home with parents. Parents often call to ask where their child is, however they could be behind the wheel unable to pickup the phone by all means. This can result in a worrying condition as the parents try to call many times but they don’t pick up the phone or it goes into voicemail..

Motivation

The motivation of this idea started when my phone kept ringing while I am driving my car. As a probationary driver, I am not allowed use any hands free device nor use anything that can pick up my phone whilst driving. Full licensed drivers on the other hand can use the hands free device such as the Bluetooth or use their car radio to pick up the phone. Therefore I wanted to create an app that allows the phone to send out a message or voicemail that lets incoming calls know that I am driving the car. This can be useful as my parents call me wondering where I am and me unable to tell them because I am driving.

Description

For this app to be developed, I intend to use react native by Facebook as I find it is a good open sourced developer for amateurs. An app name that I would call this app is Deworrying as the name suggests for parents to stop worrying where their child is at the moment. I intend to make the app run on ios 11 or later android 9 or later. As this is a medium to moderate memory using app, I would suggest this app to use 512mb or ram and 1gb of storage in order to the app to run. This app would use java frame work as well.
